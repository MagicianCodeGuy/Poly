About

North Texas Polygraph Services, LLC is owned by Stacy Shortes, a licensed polygraph examiner and retired police officer with over 23 years of law enforcement experience. During his tenure as a police officer he has been named officer of the year, has received a master marksman award, numerous commendations, and has received four life-saving awards. He has administered over ten thousand polygraph examinations, consisting of examinations for public safety agencies (pre-employment, criminal, and internal), post-conviction sex offender testing, Texas Board of Nursing, and private attorneys. He has provided polygraph examination services for over 50 public safety agencies throughout North Texas. He is a member of the Texas Association of Law Enforcement Polygraph Investigators (TALEPI), Texas Association of Polygraph Examiners (TAPE), and the American Polygraph Association (APA). North Texas Polygraph Services values trust, respect, and is committed to excellence in service.
